A Century of Maritime Excellence

Black Pearl Shipyard, established over a century ago in Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu, has been a cornerstone of India's maritime industry. From humble beginnings as a regional dock, it has grown into a world-class facility.

Over the decades, we have constructed and maintained naval, commercial, and industrial vessels, earning a reputation for precision and innovation. Our expertise spans from high-capacity cargo ships and defense vessels to custom-built luxury yachts.

With a commitment to technological advancement and sustainable practices, our shipyard integrates cutting-edge engineering solutions to enhance vessel performance and efficiency, ensuring every project meets the highest global maritime standards.

Today, Black Pearl Shipyard stands as a symbol of heritage and progress, blending tradition with modernity to build vessels that define the future of maritime exploration.
